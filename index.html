Weitong Chen is a lecturer in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. He received his bachelor degree in Geographic Science, master degree in Marine Geography and Ph.D. degree in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems from Nanjing Normal University in 2014, 2017 and 2021, respectively. In 2019, he studied in Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis at University of Oulu as a visiting researcher. His research interests include remote sensing, data security, watermark schemes, encryption algorithms, software security, etc. He has published about 13 papers in international journals.
